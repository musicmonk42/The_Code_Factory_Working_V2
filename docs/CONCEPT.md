# Project Concept

A.S.E. autonomously transforms natural-language requirements into production-ready, self-healing applications through multi-agent AI orchestration, compliance enforcement, and distributed ledger provenance.

1. **Autonomous** — self-generating, self-healing, self-evolving

- Not a replacement for human judgment — augments and automates, with guardrails and human-in-the-loop clarification
